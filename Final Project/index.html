Kensington, Maryland - Washington DC Temple

9900 Stoneybrook Dr. Kensington ,MD 20895-3199


On March 3, 2018, the Washington D.C. Temple closed for an extensive renovation project. The temple's mechanical and electrical systems will be upgraded, and all finishes and furnishings will be refreshed. A minor addition to the exterior will house stairs and a new elevator system. Changes will also be made to the grounds. The temple is anticipated to reopen in 2020, following a public open house and rededication ceremony.
